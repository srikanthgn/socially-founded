🛂 The SociallyFounded Passport

PASSPORT

SociallyFounded

Your SociallyFounded Passport is your digital entrepreneurial identity - documenting your journey, validating your commitment, and connecting you to the global community of founders.

Every check-in, contribution, and milestone is recorded in your passport, creating a visible record of your founder journey.

Digital & Physical Integration

Your passport exists both digitally in the app and physically as a premium booklet that collects stamps at venues and events.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my physical passport?

After signing up, your digital passport is immediately active. The physical passport will be sent to founding members as part of our launch program.

Do I need to pay to join SociallyFounded?

Basic membership is free. Premium features and physical passport options will be available with tiered membership plans after launch.

How do I check in at venues?

The SF app uses your location to identify nearby venues. Simply tap "Check In" when you arrive, or scan the venue's QR code where available.

What if there are no SF venues near me?

You can suggest local venues to join the network, or use the "Independent Check-in" feature at any suitable workspace.

How do investors see my passport?

You control the visibility of your passport. You can choose to make it discoverable to verified investors or share it directly with specific connections.

When is SociallyFounded launching?
